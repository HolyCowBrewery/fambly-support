Quick answers

How do family members join my tournament?

As the organizer, create a tournament and share its 6-character invite code. Anyone in the family can open Fambly on their own device, tap “Join with Code,” and enter it. Everyone then sees the same live scoreboard.

Do we each need an account?

No. You can play as a guest — just pick a display name. Sign in with Apple is optional and simply keeps your tournaments tied to you across devices.

Who can enter results?

Only the organizer enters official results, changes events, or deletes a tournament. Everyone else follows along live.

How do I delete a tournament or my data?

An organizer can delete a tournament inside the app, which removes it for all members. For account deletion requests, email us at the address above.
